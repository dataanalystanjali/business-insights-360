Business Insights 360 - Power BI Project
**Project Overview**

Business Insights 360 is an end-to-end business intelligence solution developed in Power BI to provide stakeholders with actionable insights across Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Executive functions.

The project addresses the challenge of fragmented reporting systems by consolidating data from multiple business domains into a centralized dashboard, enabling data-driven decision-making and improved business performance monitoring.

**Company Overview**

AtliQ Hardware is a rapidly growing consumer electronics and hardware manufacturing company operating across multiple markets and regions.

As the company expanded, traditional Excel-based reporting became inefficient for analyzing large volumes of business data. Decision-makers required a scalable and interactive reporting solution to monitor organizational performance across departments.

**Problem Statement**

The organization faced several reporting challenges:

Manual report preparation consuming significant time
Lack of centralized reporting
Delayed business decisions due to fragmented data sources
Difficulty tracking KPIs across departments
Limited visibility into customer, product, and market performance

The objective was to build a unified analytics platform that would provide a comprehensive view of business performance and support strategic decision-making.

**Project Objectives**

The primary objectives of this project were:

Develop a centralized business intelligence solution
Analyze sales and profitability performance
Monitor financial health of the organization
Evaluate marketing effectiveness
Improve supply chain visibility
Enable executive-level strategic reporting
Track key performance indicators across business functions
